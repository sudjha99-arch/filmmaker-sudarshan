Approach

Approach

I look for moments that are often missed
in the urgency of everyday life,
and allow them to unfold without interruption.

Observation

Stories are not constructed.
They exist around us, waiting to be noticed.

Intent

I try not to impose my own lens on the image.
The story should arrive in its purest form.

Form

Life as it is.
A slight movement of a curtain,
the faint sound of birds.
